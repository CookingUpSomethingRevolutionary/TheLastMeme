# The Last Meme

## What is this?
A custom Star Wars-style opening crawl about "The Last Meme." It's a short, humorous web project inspired by the iconic Star Wars intro.

## Features
- ⭐ Authentic Star Wars-style opening crawl
- 🎵 Classic cinematic feel
- 😂 Meme-themed story

## How to Use
1. Open the project in your browser.
2. Press **Space** to begin.
3. Enjoy!

## Controls
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Space | Start the intro |

## Technologies Used
- HTML
- CSS

## The Crawl

It is a period of dark silence. The year is 2126. The tyrannical United States Government has completely banned all digital jokes, image macros, and funny videos to save humanity from "Brainrot," a deadly mental disease spread by excessive internet usage. While the strict ban successfully cured the population's minds, it stripped the country of all happiness. Homeland Security droids now patrol the streets, enforcing mandatory serious faces and scanning for illegal smirks. Deep in the underground network of Ohio, a small band of freedom fighters risks everything to keep the spirit of comedy alive. They have spent years code-breaking an ancient, encrypted hard drive to recover the legendary, forbidden internet archive.Tonight, a lone rebel hacker prepares to broadcast the final surviving piece of humor across the entire national defense grid: the sacred image of a confused orange cat...
